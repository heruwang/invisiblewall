Invisible Wall

Heru Wang

Medium: digital media (maya, blender, A-frame, three.js)

Date: 10/5/2020

Artist Statement: This piece reflects the emotion of immigrants who are separated from their family members because of their visa restrictions. They are trapped in a place where they could hear the voice from their family, however, they could not get out. The floating texts are quotes from immigration family as they deeply miss their family yet unable to see them for a long time. Borders between countries have formed this invisible wall like a cage that restricts people’s freedom and keeps families from reuniting.
